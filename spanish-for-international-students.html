Spanish for international school students

International school students often need support that combines practical communication with the grammar and vocabulary required by their school curriculum.

Personalized lessons can reinforce classroom topics, help with homework, improve pronunciation and prepare students for speaking, reading, listening and writing assessments.

Lessons are adapted to the learner’s age, level and school goals. This helps students participate more confidently in class and use Spanish beyond memorized answers.
