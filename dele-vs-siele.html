DELE vs SIELE: what parents should know

DELE and SIELE are official Spanish proficiency qualifications, but they work differently. DELE certifies a specific CEFR level and the certificate does not expire. SIELE is completed digitally and provides a score that reflects the candidate’s current ability.

The best choice depends on the student’s age, school requirements, level and future plans. Some learners need a formal level certificate, while others prefer a flexible digital assessment.

Preparation can include speaking practice, vocabulary development, listening, reading and writing. A personalized study plan helps the student focus on the areas that need the most improvement.
