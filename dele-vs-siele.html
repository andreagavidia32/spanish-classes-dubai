DELE vs SIELE: what parents should know

DELE and SIELE are official Spanish proficiency exams, but they are not exactly the same. DELE certifies a specific level, while SIELE is more flexible and digital.

Preparation should include speaking practice, vocabulary, listening, reading and writing. The best option depends on the student’s age, level and academic goals.
